Act 3, Scene 1

Characters (in order of appearance)

Juliet - The daughter of Lord Capulet
Romeo - The son and heir of Lord Montague
Friar - A Franciscan friar and friend to both Romeo and Juliet
Benvolio - Romeo's cousin and friend
Mercutio - A kinsman to Romeo and his closest friend
Tybalt - Juliet's cousin and nephew to Lord Capulet
Boy - A page

In this scene...

Tybalt accuses Romeo of being a villain and challenges him to a duel.
Romeo and Juliet have been married in secret, so Tybalt and Romeo are family now.
Romeo refuses to fight and begs him to make peace between them.
Tybalt doesn't understand why Romeo won't fight him and continues to abuse him.
Mercutio is ashamed of Romeo's weakness and challenges Tybalt to a duel.
Romeo tries to break up the fight between Tybalt and his best friend.
However, because of his interference, Tybalt manages to stab Mercutio.
Mercutio realizes he is dying and curses both Tybalt and Romeo with his final words.
